Relief Goods Distribution

Good day, citizens! Please be informed that there will be a community gathering on July 5, 2026, to distribute relief goods. This initiative is dedicated to supporting all residents who are facing financial difficulties due to pandemic-related job losses. We strongly encourage everyone to attend, as this assistance is available to all eligible citizens.

Reminder:

During the distribution, Please keep in mind the following guidelines to ensure a smooth process: take only one relief package per household, maintain social distancing, and wear face masks at all times. Your cooperation is greatly appreaciated in making this event safe and successful for everyone involved.
